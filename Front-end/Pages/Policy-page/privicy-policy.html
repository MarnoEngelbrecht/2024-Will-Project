Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to EpicEstates! This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our property listing services on EpicEstates.com. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, list a property, or contact us, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and property details.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps of visits.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To operate and maintain our property listing services, including facilitating property listings, communications, and transactions.
  • Personalization: To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content and property recommendations based on your preferences.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information:

  • With Service Providers: To facilitate services on our behalf, such as hosting, payment processing, and customer support.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights and interests.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and use.

Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your account and information through your account settings. You may also contact us to exercise your rights regarding your personal data.
